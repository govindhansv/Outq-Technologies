What outq offer

Outq is a comprehensive and user-friendly appointments scheduling solution designed to streamline the booking process for your salon, spa, or beauty business. This advanced system not only facilitates seamless reservation management on your website but also significantly reduces the time spent on agenda management tasks, allowing you to focus more on delivering exceptional services to your clients.

  • Effortless Appointment Scheduling
  • Automated Reminders and Notifications
  • Personalized Offers and Promotions
  • Client Management and History

Special Features

Outq's features are designed to help you improve your time management skills and become a better organized person. Organize your tasks, schedule your appointments.

  • Effortless Booking

    Say goodbye to long waiting times and let customers book your salon appointments with just a few clicks. App let them Choose their preferred stylist, select services, and pick a time that suits their best.

  • Automated Reminders and Notifications

    Automated email and SMS reminders ensure that clients never miss an appointment, reducing no-shows and cancellations. These reminders can be customized to reflect your brand's voice and style.

  • Personalized Offers and Promotions

    With Outq, you can send tailored offers directly to your customers, boosting client retention and increasing bookings during off-peak times. These promotions can be based on clients' booking history, special occasions, or seasonal trends, ensuring relevance and high engagement.

  • Client Management and History

    Maintain detailed records of your clients, including their appointment history, preferences, and special notes. This information helps in providing a personalized experience, which can enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty.

  • Real-Time Availability and Calendar Syncing

    The system displays real-time availability, allowing clients to see open slots and book immediately. Integrated calendar syncing ensures that all appointments are up-to-date across all your devices and platforms.

  • Resource and Staff Management

    Manage your resources and staff efficiently by assigning appointments based on availability and skill set. This ensures optimal utilization of your team and resources, improving overall productivity.
